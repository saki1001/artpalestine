Writing a Post

To make a new entry on our blog, click here: Make a New Post

To make a hyperlink:

1. Copy the web page address that you want to link to. The easiest way to do this is to highlight the link (something like http://www.google.com) and hit COMMAND-C.

2. Highlight the text that you want people to click on.

3. Click on the little chain button:

4. Where it says 'Link URL', enter the web address that you want people to go to. Do this by pasting the web address into this window, using COMMAND-V to paste.
